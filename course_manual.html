Course manual

The current course is intended to be re-used by others. This re-use has two aspects:

  • use of the structure and part of the material of the course to develop a similar course, for a different discipline (with its own virtual laboratory)
  • use of the course (in its current form, or adapted to a different discipline) for teaching

This course manual (download will be available soon) mainly focuses on the first aspects, but we also share our experiments in how this online course is used in the blended course Learn the Scientific Method in a changing climate.
